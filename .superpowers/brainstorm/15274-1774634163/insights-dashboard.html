Insights Design

Hero Stat: Total Focus Time
The biggest number on the page is total focus time — not session count, not streak. Because time spent focusing is the truest measure of effort. The comparison ("↑ 45 min more than last week") is always framed positively — if it's down, it says "Let's build on that this week" not "down 30%."
GitHub-style Heatmap
4-week heatmap with intensity = sessions per day. Familiar pattern (every dev knows this from GitHub). Empty cells are neutral, not red. Shows patterns: "I focus more on Tuesdays" is actionable insight. TODAY is outlined to show where you are.
Milestone Journey
Horizontal scroll of milestone badges: 3, 5, 7, 14, 30, 60, 100 days. Achieved ones glow with violet. Upcoming ones show "X to go." After a broken streak: the journey doesn't reset — achieved milestones stay achieved. "Starting fresh 🌱" replaces the streak but past milestones remain.
Mood Trend Bars
Daily average mood as bar height + gradient color. Green = great days, violet = good, amber = okay, low bar = hard. A quick visual pulse of the week. Average mood shown as a summary word, not a cold number.
Session Log
Today's sessions with time, task name, duration, and mood icon. A personal timeline of effort. Seeing "3 sessions, 47 minutes" in concrete form is deeply validating for ADHD brains that often feel like they "did nothing all day."
Data Model (localStorage)
Everything stored locally, no backend required.
sessions[] {date, startTime, duration, task, mood, completed}
streaks {current, longest, lastActiveDate}
milestones[] {target, achievedDate}
settings {defaultDuration, breathingEnabled, soundEnabled}
today computed from sessions[] on load
