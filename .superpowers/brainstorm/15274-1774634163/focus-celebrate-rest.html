Focus Mode

Task Anchoring
Your intention from the home screen stays visible at top. When your ADHD brain asks "wait, what was I doing?" — the answer is right there.
Ambient Encouragement
Subtle rotating messages appear: "You're doing great," "Almost halfway," "Stay with it." Never intrusive — just a gentle anchor. Toggleable in settings.
End Early ≠ Failure
"End Early" button is there without judgment. If you focused for 8 of 25 minutes, that still counts as a session. The celebration screen still fires.
Wake Lock API
Screen stays on during focus. No accidental lock. The timer is always visible without touching the phone.

Celebration

Confetti + Sound
Particle burst (canvas-based, performant) + a warm chime. The celebration is 3-4 seconds — long enough to feel rewarding, short enough not to annoy.
Rotating Messages
Pool of 30+ messages: "You showed up. That's everything." / "Your brain needed that. Well done." / "Small wins build big things." Never repeats within a day.
Mood Check-In: SVG Faces
5 options with SVG face icons (Hard / Okay / Good / Great / Flow). One tap, no friction. Mood data feeds the Insights dashboard. The "Flow" option captures those rare magical sessions.
Partial Sessions Count
Even if you ended early, you get the celebration. A 5-minute session after 3 days of nothing deserves confetti.

Rest Mode

Color Shift
Entire palette shifts from violet to emerald green. Subconscious signal: different mode, different energy. The transition is a 700ms crossfade.
"You've earned this"
Framing rest as an achievement, not laziness. ADHD users often feel guilty about breaks. This language reframes rest as part of the process.
Break Suggestions
Physical micro-activities: stretch, look out window, get water. Encourages actually leaving the desk — ADHD rest ≠ scrolling phone. Suggestions rotate each session.
Break Duration
Auto-calculated: 5 min break after 5/15-min sessions, 10 min after 25-min, 15 min after every 4th session (long break). Skippable.
