Screen Details

🏠

Home / Ready State

What you see when you open the app. Shows: greeting message (time-aware), today's stats at a glance (sessions done, streak count), the timer ring set to your chosen duration, a big "Start Focus" button, and a small link to the Dashboard. Also: quick duration picker (5 / 15 / 25 / custom min) and an optional "What are you working on?" text field.

🫁

Pre-Session Ritual (Optional, Skippable)

A 10-second breathing animation: "Breathe in... hold... breathe out..." with an expanding/contracting circle. Purpose: create a mental transition between "I'm scrolling" and "I'm working." Always skippable — ADHD users hate forced gates. Fades into the timer automatically.

⏱️

Focus Mode (Timer Running)

The existing timer, enhanced: dimmed background (ambient mode), the SVG ring counting down, your task name visible at top, and a subtle pulsing glow. Pause/Reset buttons available. Optional: a gentle mid-session chime at the halfway point as an anchor. Screen stays awake via Wake Lock API.

🎉

Celebration Screen (Post-Session)

A 3-4 second celebration moment: particle/confetti burst animation, a rotating encouraging message ("You just did something hard. That matters."), session count increment animation, and a 1-tap mood check-in (5 emoji options: 😤😔😐🙂🔥). Then auto-transitions into rest timer.

📊

Dashboard (Stats View)

A separate view (slide-up panel or page) showing: today's sessions with mood + task names, current streak with milestone badges (3, 7, 14, 30 days), a weekly focus heatmap (7 columns, intensity = session count), total lifetime focus hours, and a mood trend mini-chart. Accessed via a small icon on the home screen.
